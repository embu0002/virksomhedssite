ABOUT

A little about my journeys and how I got here:

I was born and raised in Iran 8th of March 1977. Just before the “Islamic Revolution” and the war between Iran and Irak that forced us to flee to Europe in the late 80ies.

Since childhood being connected to God and the spiritual world has been as necessary as breathing for me. But because of a growing Islamaphobia and colonization I parked that connection until I turned 20. Poetry and writing was my first love and where I unknowingly could connect to God.
At 20 I was guided by the spiritual world to travel to London. On the streets of Brixton marked I met a friend who became one of my mentors who took me in and helped me reconnect to my self and the spiritual world. Back than he was Tony. Today he is known as Mooji world wide.

Only 24 years old I opened Beat Root with my mother. One of the most loved food places in Copenhagen. Same year I was guided through a friend to join this NGO called RC, where I still am very active leading and teaching. Same year I got pregnant unexpectedly and had my daughter at 25. She is a magical being who has changed and upgraded me and my life to a level I never could imagine. I have been a single parent most of her life.

In my late 20is I had a melt down with stress and anxiety. It took me 3 years to heal from that with various tools, like acupuncture, herbal medicine, ACA meetings and loads of rest. At 31 I opened Kimia. A total game changing cafe/bar/ venue in Copenhagen. A safe space and platform for women, biopics and LGBTQA+. Kimia closed after 10 years, the day after my birthday March 9th 2021. The year before that I had started my education in healing and clairvoyance. I can’t even explain how this journey elevated me, my life and the lives of my surroundings.

Working as an acupuncturist, clairvoyant, healer and a spiritual teacher is my true calling in this life and I know that because I am the most happy, joyful, hopeful in my core when I work.
My work is pure gold! Pure Magic! And I am so so in love with it!
